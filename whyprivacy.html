Why should you protect your privacy?

Your personal data is more than just numbers, it’s part of who you are.
In our connected world, this information is valuable to companies and cybercriminals who might use it without your approval.
By protecting your privacy, you:
Avoid Identity Theft: Keeping personal details safe reduces the risk of fraud.
Stay in Control: You decide what to share and who sees it.
Prevent Manipulation: Fewer trackers mean fewer targeted ads or hidden influences on your decisions.
Keep Your Life Private: You can express yourself freely without being watched or judged.
Defend Your Rights: Privacy is a basic human right, and protecting it helps you stay secure and independent online.
Simple actions, like using strong passwords, limiting data on social media, or learning about privacy tools—can make a big difference. Start now to reclaim your power and keep what’s truly yours, yours.
